What is an Intranet?

Intranet

An intranet is a private network that is used within an organization to share information, resources, and communication among employees. It is typically accessible only to authorized users within the organization and is not available to the general public. Intranets can include features such as internal websites, document sharing, collaboration tools, and communication platforms. They are designed to improve communication, collaboration, and productivity within an organization by providing a centralized platform for employees to access information and resources.
